Iterator Project:
Songs Edge

Collaborative file compendium for the Iterator Project(s) Veil Of Dreams, Visions Of The Past and Four Blue Flames.

The following document contains information on the aforementioned Iterators that includes initialisation logs, observations, notes and audio transcripts.

Logs are maintained by Iterator Architects and Administrators and are viewable to the full Iterator care team.

This serves for cataloguing purposes only and is overseen by the Intercontinental Iterator Committee.

Songs Edge Local Group is located in in the Northern Stripe, Sector: Four White Bones Mountains. First established in 1114.102 as a mining facility.

Project Brief - Songs Edge aims to (above all) contribute to the research efforts towards the Great Problem; with our 6 Iterators working tirelessly on the Solution with additional specialisations in a multitude of fields.

Songs Edge consists of Iterators: Visions Of The Past, Veil Of Dreams, Quick Silver, Ongoing Encore, Four Blue Flames and Two Birds.

The Local Group also serves as a crossing to the neighboring sector Soft Snow Valley and for communications management with the nearby hydroelectric facility.
